Main ToolBar
This toolbar switch different objects and states of the rig.

1 - Show/Hide Posers
2 - Show/Hide Controllers
3 - Show/Hide Joints
4 - Show/Hide Geometry
5 - Joints template state  
6 - Geometry template state 
7 - Geometry reference state
8 - Show/Hide Posers Axises
9 - Show/Hide Joints Axises
